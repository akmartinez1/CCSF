Programming Exercise A
Write a program which can check a password to see if
it is in the ACRC/hills initial password format, i.e.
mmmDDYY.fl, where:
• mmm - three lowercase characters for month, e.g.
"mar" is March
• DD - two digits for day
• YY - two digits for year
• . - a period
• fl - two lowercase characters for first and last initials
